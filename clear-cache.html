Clear Cache - 241 Runners Awareness

Use this page to clear all caches and force fresh content to load.

Manual Steps:

  1. Open Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to Application tab
  3. Click on "Storage" in the left sidebar
  4. Click "Clear site data"
  5. Check all boxes and click "Clear site data"
  6. Go to "Service Workers" in the left sidebar
  7. Click "Unregister" for any service workers
  8. Hard refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
