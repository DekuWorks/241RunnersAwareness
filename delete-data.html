Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of specific types of data from your 241 Runners Awareness account. This allows you to remove certain information while keeping your account active.

What Data Can Be Deleted

  • Profile Information: Personal details, contact information, and profile settings
  • Cases & Reports: Missing person reports and case-related data you've submitted
  • Notification History: Past notifications and communication records
  • Location History: Location tracking data and running route information
  • Messages & Communications: Chat messages and communication logs
  • App Preferences & Settings: Customized app settings and preferences

üìã Data Retention Policy

  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes
  • Processing may take up to 30 days
  • You will receive confirmation once deletion is complete
  • Data that cannot be deleted will be explained in the response

How to Request Data Deletion

1
Log into your account

Access the 241 Runners Awareness app or website and log in with your credentials.

2
Navigate to Privacy Policy

Go to the Privacy Policy page and scroll to the "Data Management Actions" section.

3
Click "Delete My Data"

Select the "Delete My Data" button to open the data deletion request form.

4
Select data types

Choose which specific types of data you want to delete using the checkboxes provided.

5
Submit your request

Optionally provide a reason for the deletion and submit your request for processing.
