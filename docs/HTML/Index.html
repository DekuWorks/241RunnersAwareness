Our Mission

241 Runners Awareness was founded to bridge the critical gaps between runaway youth, their families, and law enforcement, with the goal of ensuring no child is lost without recognition or support. Inspired by the tragic story of Israel Thomas, a young man who was misidentified following a fatal accident, 241 Runners Awareness is committed to meaningful change for adoptive families affected by runaway cases.
