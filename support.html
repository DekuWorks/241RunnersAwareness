Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a missing person? ‚ñº

To report a missing person, visit our Report Case page and fill out the required information. For emergency situations, please contact local law enforcement immediately at 911.

How do I download the mobile app? ‚ñº

The 241 Runners Awareness mobile app is available for download on the App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android). Search for "241 Runners Awareness" in your device's app store.

Is my personal information secure? ‚ñº

Yes, we take data security seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with privacy regulations and never share your information without consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I update my account information? ‚ñº

You can update your account information by logging into your account and visiting the Profile section. For mobile app users, go to Settings > Profile to make changes.

What should I do if I can't log in? ‚ñº

If you're having trouble logging in, try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If the issue persists, contact our support team at contact@241runnersawareness.org.

How do I volunteer with 241 Runners Awareness? ‚ñº

We welcome volunteers! Please contact us at volunteer@241runnersawareness.org or visit our About Us page to learn more about volunteer opportunities and how you can help our mission.
